Pizza Meatballs

Prep/Total Time: 10 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 1/4 cup dried minced onion
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • Pizza sauce
  • 6 meatballs
  • 8 ounces cubed part-skim mozzarella cheese, 1/2 inch cubes
  • 2 pizza slices

Preparation

  1. Stuff a cube of cheese on the meatball.
  2. Add onion, garlic, pepper and pizza sauce. Serve with pizza.
  3. Microwave in 2 minutes intervals
  4. Enjoy!
