Healthier Instant Noodles In A Jar

Prep/Total Time: 5 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon toasted sesame oil
  • pepper, to taste
  • ¼ cup cucumber, thinly sliced
  • ¼ cup carrot, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 cup dry instant noodle, such as vermicelli
  • ½ boiled egg
  • 1 cup hot broth, or hot water

Preparation

  1. Fill a mason jar with soy sauce, sesame oil, pepper, cucumbers, carrots, noodles, and egg.
  2. Adjust seasonings to taste.
  3. Store in the fridge for up to 5 days.
  4. When you are ready to serve, pour in the hot water or broth. Stir well.
  5. Enjoy!
