Microwave Egg Custard Pudding

Prep/Total Time: 15 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp Sugar

Preparation

  1. n a microwave safe jug heat the milk for 3 minutes on high.
  2. In a bowl beat the eggs,sugar whisk into the milk.
  3. Stirring all the time strain this mix into a dish. Cook on 1/2 power for 4 minutes. Stir and then cook on 1/2 power for another 5 minutes.
  4. Stand 5 minutes.
  5. Enjoy!
