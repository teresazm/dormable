Microwaved Caprese Pita Pizza

Prep/Total Time:3 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 1 pita
  • 4 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 3 slices tomato
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • fresh Basil, optional

Preparation

  1. Top a pita with mozzarella, tomato, salt, and pepper.
  2. Microwave for one minute.
  3. Serve with fresh basil.
  4. Enjoy!
