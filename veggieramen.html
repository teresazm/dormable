Cheese Veggie Noodles

Prep/Total Time: 10 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 1 package instant noodles
  • ¼ cup milk
  • 1 cup chopped frozen veggie
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • ¼ cup shredded cheddar cheese

Preparation

  1. Heat oil a large skillet over medium heat. Add prepared instant noodles to pan.
  2. Stir in milk, then cheese. Stir in frozen; season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve.
  3. Enjoy!
