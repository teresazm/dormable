ABOUT

DORMABLE provides college students with recipe websites dedicated to generating new food combinations with leftover. College students usually do not have a kitchen nor appliances in the dorms, so most of the kitchen supplies that can be used are kettles and microwaves. Through DORMABLE, you can solve a meal quickly and easily, and save money.
