ABOUT DORMABLE

DORMABLE dedicates to providing college students with recipes website dedicated to generating new food for leftover combinations. College students usually do not have a kitchen in the dorms, and most of the kitchen supplies that can be used are kettles and microwaves. Through DORMABLE, you can solve a meal quickly and easily, and save money.
