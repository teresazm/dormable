Basic Microwave Scrambled Eggs

Prep/Total Time: 3 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp ( 30 mL ) milk
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper

Preparation

  1. Spray microwave-safe container (e.g. mug, ramekin, or egg cooker) with cooking spray or wipe lightly with vegetable oil.
  2. Whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper in container (or whisk ingredients in another bowl and pour into microwave container). If using a mug or ramekin, cover with plastic wrap, pulling back small area for venting. If using an egg cooker, place lid on cooker base, lining up notches. Twist to secure.
  3. Microwave on Medium-High (70% power) for 1 minute and 30 seconds to 1 minute and 45 seconds, stirring several times during cooking.
  4. Cover and let stand for 30 seconds to 1 minute before serving. Eggs will look slightly moist, but will finish cooking upon standing.
  5. Enjoy!
