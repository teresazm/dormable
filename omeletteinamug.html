Microwave 3-minute Omelette In A Mug

Prep/Total Time: 3 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 slices ham, diced
  • ½ bell pepper, diced
  • ¼ cup frozen veggie
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste

Preparation

  1. Combine all ingredients in a microwaveable mug.
  2. Cook for 2-3 minutes, making sure the egg doesn’t bubble over. Stir halfway through the cooking process.
  3. Enjoy!
