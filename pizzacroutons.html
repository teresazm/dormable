Crunchy Leftover Pizza Croutons

Prep/Total Time: 15min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 2 slices of leftover pizza
  • frozen veggie
  • Vegetable oil

Preparation

  1. Begin heating the tomato soup and remove the crust from your leftover pizza. Place one slice over the other so the cheese is on the inside. Cut into 1-inch squares as best you can.
  2. Coat a Air fryer with a healthy amount of vegetable oil and fry croutons for about 3 minutes or until the cheese between the slices melts and the bottom crust is golden brown.
  3. Flip each crouton and airfry for another 3 minutes or until they are completely golden brown and crunchy.
  4. Mix frozen veggie, salt, paper and water into microwave bowl, Microwave on Medium-High (70% power) for 10 minute, stirring several times during cooking.
  5. Drain the croutons on paper towel and toss over Veggie soup, salad or snack on them alone.
  6. Enjoy!
