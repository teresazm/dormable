Bacon And Cheese In A Mug

Prep/Total Time: 10 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 1 slice bread
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 pinch cheddar cheese
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • frozen veggie, to taste
  • green onion, minced

Preparation

  1. Slice bread into cubes.
  2. In a bowl, add egg, milk, cheese, salt, and pepper, and mix.
  3. In a microwave-safe mug, place half of the cubed bread and desired amount of frozen veggie.
  4. Place the rest of the bread and fixins and pour the egg mixture into the mug.
  5. Microwave for 1Â½ minutes or until egg is fully cooked.
  6. Sprinkle with some green onions.
  7. Enjoy!
