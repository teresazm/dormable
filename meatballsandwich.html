Meatball Sub Sandwich

Prep/Total Time: 5 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 3 meatballs
  • 2 bread
  • Â¼ cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Preparation

  1. In a microwave-safe meatball sub sandwich, cheese, salt, pepper, and egg.
  2. Microwave on high for 90 seconds, until the bread has risen.
  3. Let the sandwich cool, then serve.
  4. Enjoy!
