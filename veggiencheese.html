Veggie And Cheese Breakfast In A Mug

Prep/Total Time: 5 min

Ingredients

For 1 serving

  • 1 slice bread
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 pinch cheddar cheese
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 small handful fresh spinach
  • frozen veggie, diced

Preparation

  1. Slice bread into cubes.
  2. In a bowl, add egg, milk, cheese, salt, frozen veggie and pepper, and mix.
  3. In a microwave-safe mug, place half of the cubed bread and desired amount of spinach and red pepper.
  4. Place the rest of the bread and mix in and pour the egg mixture into the mug.
  5. Microwave for 1Â½ minutes or until egg is fully cooked.
  6. Drizzle maple syrup.
  7. Enjoy!
